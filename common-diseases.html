Common Diseases

Migraine

A neurological disorder causing severe headaches, nausea, and sensitivity to light/sound.

  • Symptoms: Intense headache, nausea, sensitivity to light and sound.
  • Causes: Stress, hormonal changes, lack of sleep, certain foods.
  • Treatment: Pain relief medication, avoiding triggers, lifestyle adjustments.

Gastritis

Inflammation of the stomach lining, leading to pain and digestive issues.

  • Symptoms: Stomach pain, nausea, bloating, vomiting.
  • Causes: Infection (H. pylori), excessive alcohol, stress, NSAIDs.
  • Treatment: Antacids, antibiotics (if bacterial), diet modifications.

Cold

The common cold is a viral infection of your nose and throat (upper respiratory tract).

  • Symptoms: Runny or stuffy nose, sore throat, cough, congestion, slight body aches, sneezing.
  • Causes: Various viruses, most commonly rhinoviruses.
  • Treatment: Rest, fluids, over-the-counter cold remedies.

Tuberculosis (TB)

A bacterial infection affecting the lungs, spread through airborne droplets.

  • Symptoms: Persistent cough, weight loss, fever, night sweats.
  • Causes: Mycobacterium tuberculosis, airborne transmission.
  • Treatment: Long-term antibiotics, isolation in active cases.

Lower Back Pain

Lower back pain is a common condition that affects the lower portion of the spine.

  • Symptoms: Muscle ache, shooting or stabbing pain, pain that radiates down the leg, limited range of motion.
  • Causes: Muscle or ligament strain, bulging or ruptured disks, arthritis, skeletal irregularities.
  • Treatment: Physical therapy, medications, rest, lifestyle modifications.

Dengue Fever

A mosquito-borne viral disease causing high fever, severe body pain, and rash.

  • Symptoms: High fever, severe body pain, rash, nausea.
  • Causes: Dengue virus, Aedes mosquito bites.
  • Treatment: Hydration, pain relievers, hospitalization in severe cases.
