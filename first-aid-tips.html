First Aid Tips

Burns

If you or someone else gets a burn, here are the steps to take:

  • Cool the burn with cool (not cold) water for at least 10 minutes.
  • Cover the burn with a sterile, non-adhesive bandage or clean cloth.
  • Avoid using ice, butter, or ointments on the burn.
  • Seek medical attention if the burn is severe.

Cuts and Scrapes

For minor cuts and scrapes, follow these steps:

  • Clean the wound with water and mild soap.
  • Apply an antibiotic ointment to prevent infection.
  • Cover the wound with a sterile bandage or gauze.
  • Change the dressing regularly to keep the wound clean.

Nosebleed

To stop a nosebleed, follow these steps:

  • Sit upright and lean slightly forward.
  • Pinch the soft part of the nose for 10-15 minutes.
  • Avoid tilting the head back or stuffing tissues inside.
  • Seek medical help if bleeding persists for more than 20 minutes.

Sprains and Strains

For a sprain or strain, use the RICE method:

  • Rest the injured area.
  • Ice it for 15-20 minutes every few hours.
  • Compress with an elastic bandage to reduce swelling.
  • Elevate the limb above heart level.
  • Seek medical care if severe pain or swelling occurs.
